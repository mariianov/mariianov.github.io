About Me

I'm a graphics reporter and data journalist pursuing a Master's degree in investigative and data journalism. I specialize in creating compelling visual narratives that make complex information accessible and engaging.

My work combines technical skills in data analysis (R programming), design (Adobe Creative Suite), and animation to tell stories that matter. I'm passionate about using visual journalism to inform communities and hold power accountable.

Currently working as a graphics reporter, I pitch and produce maps, charts, explainers, and illustrations while collaborating with reporters and editors on data-driven investigations.
